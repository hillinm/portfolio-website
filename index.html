Hi. I'm Mark Hillin.

I am a Geospatial Analyst. Geospatial Analysis refers to the use and analysis of geospatial information to assess geographically referenced activities on Earth as geospatial intelligence (GEOINT). Learn about what I do

GEOINT consists of:

Knowing the Earth, Showing the Way, And Understanding the World.

Imagery

A likeness of any natural or man-made feature, as well as its location.

Imagery Intelligence (IMINT)

Information derived through interpreting imagery.

Geospatial Information

Information that identifies a natural or constructed feature on Earth by its geographic location and other characteristics.

A picture is worth a thousand words. Every day satellites in the sky capture the comings and goings of nations around the world. These images may provide the missing piece to the puzzle that can help keep the nation safe.
